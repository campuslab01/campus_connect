# Instamojo KYC Compliance Note

**To:** Instamojo Compliance Team
**Subject:** KYC Verification & Business Model Clarification for Campus Connect

## 1. Business Overview
**Campus Connect** is a social discovery and professional networking platform designed exclusively for verified university students in India. Our mission is to help students build meaningful connections, find mentors, and expand their social network in a safe, verified environment.

**We are NOT a dating agency, escort service, or adult content platform.** We are a student networking community.

## 2. Target Audience & Verification
- **Strictly 18+**: Our platform is open ONLY to individuals aged 18 years and above.
- **Student Verification**: Users must provide valid college details.
- **Identity Check**: We employ a strict age confirmation process during onboarding.

## 3. Compliance with Indian Regulations
We strictly adhere to the **Information Technology Act, 2000** and the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**.

- **Terms & Conditions**: Our T&C (accessible at `/terms`) explicitly prohibits:
  - Adult/Pornographic content
  - Solicitation or prostitution
  - Illegal activities
  - Hate speech or harassment
- **Privacy Policy**: Our Privacy Policy (accessible at `/privacy`) outlines clear data collection, storage, and grievance redressal mechanisms as per Indian law.
- **Grievance Officer**: We have a dedicated mechanism for reporting and resolving user complaints.

## 4. Platform Safety & Risk Mitigation
To ensure a safe environment and zero risk of illegal activities:
- **Zero Tolerance Policy**: Any user found sharing explicit content or soliciting commercial sexual services is immediately banned.
- **Reporting Mechanism**: Users can report profiles and content directly within the app.
- **Moderation**: We employ community guidelines to maintain a professional and respectful atmosphere.

## 5. Monetization Model
Our revenue comes from **Premium Subscriptions** (SaaS model) which offer:
- Advanced filtering options (by department, college).
- Profile visibility boosts.
- Ad-free experience.

**We do NOT facilitate or process payments for:**
- Physical dates or meetings.
- Adult services or content.
- Gambling or gaming.

## 6. Refund & Cancellation
Our Refund and Cancellation policy is clearly defined in our Terms & Conditions. We comply with consumer protection laws regarding digital goods and subscriptions.
